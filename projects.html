Dam Koto?

This extension adds a price tag in native currency selected beside the current price tags of amazon's price tags. Ever went to Amazon looking for things to buy or maybe to see how much a item costs compared to your country's price? Wasn't it painful and time consuming to convert those prices to your native currency? Had to pull the live currency rate and use a calculator to calculate the price in your native currency and repeat the same process each time you wanted to check a price. Here is where "Dam Koto?" comes in. You just sit back and browse the items and let "Dam Koto?" do all the work for you. Dam koto adds converted price tags beside the Amazon's price tags. No need to worry anymore, you just enjoy your shopping.
